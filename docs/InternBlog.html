This Blog Is Dedicated To My Summer 2024 Internship At ECCCA

Week of May 13th

This starting week I got introduced to the staff across each job site, including Erie Bayfront Convention Center, Erie Insurance Arena, UPMC Ball Park, Warner Theater, and the Sheraton Hotel. I collaborated with the website manager, social media team, and production manager. We started a big project setting up around 50 cameras at the Sheraton. In downtime, I revived this website, combining school projects and cleaning up pages.

Week of May 20th

Got to know the Sheraton team better. Hung about 20 cameras. I updated my website’s color scheme to a clean, classic color palette and scheduled a meeting with Erica about younger audience engagement and anime expo/pokemon tournament ideas.

Week of August 5th

My final reflection week — learned a ton about office workflow, project collaboration, cybersecurity, web limitations, and content creation with Adobe Suite. Developed real appreciation for how supportive and fast-paced professional environments can be. Great relationships made and valuable lessons learned for post-grad work life.
